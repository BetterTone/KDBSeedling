Musical Theatre Classes

The heart of our studio is our musical theatre program.
If your child has Broadway on the brain and wants to do it all - singing, dancing, drama - then our Musical Theatre classes are just the ticket.
Our school-year semester musical theatre classes (Fall and Spring) produce youth editions of Broadway shows, edited for content, length, and vocal performance levels.
Summer musicals may include full Broadway productions with audition-only casts. No experience is necessary, and vocals and choreography for each class are tailored to age range and skill level.

Acting Classes

Don't let all the theatre jargon fool you. These are super fun classes!
For those wishing to focus more on the dramatic facet of their performance skill set, our acting classes expand knowledge of stagecraft and provide opportunities for practical application through games and activities in addition to weekly rehearsals for that semester's play or short scene collection.
Courses are offered for Juniors (Age 7-10) and Teens (Age 11-18), and include:
  • Exploration of basic stage acting skills
  • Theatre terminology and etiquette
  • Vocal inflection and projection
  • Movement and facial expression
  • A variety of techniques and acting methods

We have a brand new
Next Level Acting Skills Class!

Improve your performance abilities - Impress at your next audition - Learn how to engage your audience more fully - Take your acting to the next level!
This one hour skill building class will focus on a specific item in the actor's toolbox each week. Class time will include interactive instruction and participation in areas such as projection, emotion, movement, characterization, improvisation and much more.
Ms. Danni brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to this awesome course that is sure to be an invaluable addition to your Spring theatre experience.

NOTE: There is no end of semester show associated with this class.

Vocal Performance Classes

Seedling consistently draws and develops incredibly talented youth vocalists, thanks in most part to our amazing singing coach, Britni Favero.
Group vocal performance classes and camps are available year-round for both beginners and advanced students, with an emphasis on solo performance.
Technical skills are taught alongside stage presence to create unforgettable recitals and personal improvement in musical theatre class performances.
Confidence is built by students performing weekly in class for their peers who provide positive feedback and support.

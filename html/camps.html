Summer Camps

 
Summer Camps vary, but generally include at least one musical production, full plays, vocal performance, movie making, camps for ages 4-6, set design and costume design, beginning acting, and much more. Explore below for specific camp information.
 
The classic Disney film comes to life onstage this summer at Seedling in this Broadway JR version, complete with all the beloved characters and songs.
There are four different musical theatre camps for June 2023: Juniors and Teens Cast camp, along with Juniors and Teens Ensemble camp.
Ensemble cast performs in all 4 shows with the Little Mermaid Cast as Sailors ("Fathoms Below"), Seagulls ("Human Stuff"), Sea creatures ("Under the Sea"), and Lagoon characters ("Kiss the Girl").

Seedling Theatre Company is committed to building
confidence, nurturing leadership abilities, and
developing communication skills for your children

Our Mission
Seedling Theatre Company is dedicated to providing an environment where children and teens feel welcome, encouraged, and empowered through participating in the performing arts and where instructors teach with patience and love, striving to develop the potential in every student.

Your Child Is in Good Hands
As wonderful as our shows are, the main goals of this theatre company are fulfilled each week in the "classroom" settings of our semester-long courses and week-long summer camps. Be it musical theatre, acting, or vocal performance, there's something here for your creative child.

Spring 2023 Shows Are Set!
Aliens, islands, jungles, and voices! The Spring 2023 show schedule is set. Our students are working hard to bring you these productions, so please come out and support them.
